The Mathematics Department at Calumpang High School is dedicated to helping students
build a strong foundation in math through
engaging and challenging lessons.
Our curriculum spans from basic arithmetic and algebra to advanced topics
such as geometry, trigonometry, and General Mathematics.
We focus on developing critical thinking,
problem-solving, and analytical skills, ensuring students gain a deep understanding
of mathematical concepts and their real-world
applications. With passionate teachers and interactive learning methods, students are encouraged to excel and appreciate the role of
math in everyday life. In addition to classroom instruction, students have the opportunity
to join math clubs and participate in competitions, further enhancing their skills and confidence.
